The Sensorimotor Neural Engineering Lab at UC Berkeley studies how sensory and motor brain signals dynamically coordinate to enable the learning and execution of fast, flexible, and dexterous behaviors. We then seek to leverage these principles to design neurotechnology that can rehabilitate and restore fluid movements in patients with injured sensorimotor networks.

The lab is opening in July 2023 and is actively recruiting -- see the "Join" tab for more details!
